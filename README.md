## 📌 About the Project

GrievAI is an Agentic AI powered grievance redressal platform that transforms traditional complaint management into an intelligent, autonomous governance system.

Unlike conventional grievance portals, GrievAI does not simply collect complaints. It understands, analyzes, prioritizes, verifies, routes, and assists users through AI-driven workflows.

The platform enables citizens to submit grievances using text, voice, images, and documents while receiving real-time support through an AI chatbot and AI voice calling agent. Advanced NLP models analyze complaint sentiment, urgency, authenticity, and context to ensure faster and more accurate resolutions.

Designed for scalability, transparency, and accessibility, GrievAI can be adopted by universities, organizations, municipalities, NGOs, and government departments.
